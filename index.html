About Me

I am a four-year Ph.D. candidate in Theoretical Mathematics at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University, supervised by Prof. Zhifei Zhang.

Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Zhejiang University in 2022, where I was advised by Prof. Ting Zhang.

Download my CV (Last updated: Nov. 30, 2025).

I co-organize long-term analysis seminars at Peking University, focusing on PDEs and geometric measure theory.
