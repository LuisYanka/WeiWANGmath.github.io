Preprints and Publications

Fine structure of rupture set for semilinear elliptic equation with singular nonlinearity
Wei Wang and Zhifei Zhang, arXiv preprint arXiv:2411.16048.
This paper shows the rectifiability of stratification structure for singular sets (rupture sets) of stationary solutions of semilinear elliptic equations with singular nonlinearity. Additionally, it gives estimates for the optimal Lorentz norms of such solutions. 		arXiv
Quantitative stratification and sharp regularity estimates for supercritical semilinear elliptic equations
Haotong Fu, Wei Wang, and Zhifei Zhang, arXiv preprint arXiv:2408.06726.
This paper studies the stratification of semilinear elliptic equations and shows the rectifiability of each stratum. It also gives sharp improvements in the regularity when the defect measure does not occur. 		arXiv
Landau-de Gennes model with sextic potentials: asymptotic behavior of minimizers
Wei Wang and Zhifei Zhang, arXiv preprint arXiv:2404.00677.
In this paper, we solve an open question raised by Canevari (ARMA, 223 (2017), 591-676), specifically concerning point and line defects in the Landau-de Gennes model with sextic potentials. 		arXiv
On the sharp constants in the regional fractional Sobolev inequalities
Rupert L. Frank, Tianling Jin, and Wei Wang, arXiv preprint arXiv:2403.00357. To appear on Minimax Theory and its Applications.
This paper considers sharp constants in fractional Sobolev inequalities associated with the regional fractional Laplacian in domains. 		arXiv
Uniform estimates of resolvents in homogenization theory of elliptic systems
Wei Wang, Journal of Differential Equations, 2023.
This paper gives the estimates of resolvents in the homogenization theory of elliptic systems, which is uniform with respect to the small parameter through the construction of Green functions. 		journal arXiv
Homogenization theory of elliptic system with lower order terms for dimension two
Wei Wang and Ting Zhang, Communications on Pure and Applied Analysis, 2022.
In this paper, we study the homogenization theory of elliptic equations with lower-order terms in 2D domains. We also provide useful tricks in analyzing 2D green functions for variable coefficients. 		journal arXiv
