About Me

My name is Wei Wang (王尉). I am currently a four-year Ph.D. candidate in Theoretical Mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Zhifei Zhang at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University.

Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from the School of Mathematical Sciences at Zhejiang University in 2022, advised by Prof. Ting Zhang.
