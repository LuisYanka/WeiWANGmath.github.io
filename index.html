About Me

I am a four-year Ph.D. candidate in Theoretical Mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Zhifei Zhang at the School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University.

Previously, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from the School of Mathematical Sciences at Zhejiang University in 2022, advised by Prof. Ting Zhang.

Analysis Seminars

I co-organize long-term analysis seminars at Peking University, focusing on partial differential equations and geometric measure theory.

  • Research areas: Geometric variational problems, elliptic equations, parabolic equations
  • Selected topics: Homogenization theory, semilinear elliptic and parabolic equations, harmonic maps, liquid crystal theory, nonlocal Laplacian operators
